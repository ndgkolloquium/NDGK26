DIRECTIONS


The meeting takes place at the Department of Mathematics of the RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau in Kaiserslautern, located in the building 48 in Gottlieb-Daimler-Straße 48.

It is about a 30 minutes walk from Kaiserslautern Main Station and 40 minutes from downtown.
The bus lines 105 and 115 travel between Kaiserslautern Main Station and the math department, stop "Kaiserslautern, Uni Zentral", and take about 10 minutes. More information is available through the VRN website.
